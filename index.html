Independent Project Proposal - Ella Ferrin

Project Name

Fun Places Website Overhaul

Overview

For my independent project, I am going to be creating a website for my mom’s small business, Fun Places. My mom, Danielle, designs and paints murals in public spaces, like elementary and secondary schools, dentist and doctors' offices, churches and more! Currently, she doesn’t have a website and the only online presence her company has is a Facebook page where she uploads images of her project. While this is good for showcasing the visuals of her previous work, it doesn’t really tell the whole story about her brand. For this reason, I will be designing a Squarespace website for her after conducting competitor and user research to determine how best to design the site.

Goals

  1. Conduct competitor and user research to understand the mural and art market as it relates to online content and promotion
  2. Establish a cohesive story about Fun Places that is well told through a Squarespace site
  3. Showcase past murals and projects for fans and potential clients to view
  4. Create a straightforward website that is well-designed, as free of unintentional bias as possible and accessible to all users of the web

Audience

Primary Persona: Potential Clients

  • Job Roles: School Principals, Pastors, Office Managers
  • Goal:To make the spaces where they work more visually appealing so that their patrons are more engaged
  • Priotities:Want to have a general understanding of the cost to determine if the project will fit their budget, want to see past examples of work to ensure visual style aligns, interested to learn more about artist to make sure values are aligned
  • Frustrations/Pain Points: Lots of businesses to choose from/highly saturated market, lack of mural customization, too high of a cost to execute, unsure of what design they want

Secondary Persona: Fans

  • Job Roles:Art Fanatics, Past Clients
  • Goal:To view past Fun Places work to get inspiration for their own work, admire the craftsmanship and engage with the artist
  • Priorities:Want to find new content that is inspirational and unique, engage with said content on social media along with a website
  • Frustrations/Pain Points:Websites that are difficult to navigate, lack of centralization of artists’ pieces of work, no easy way to reshare work to social media or personal storage
